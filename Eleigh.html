Australasia

Culture:

The culture of Austrilasia is essentially the culture of Australia. It originated from there, as it is the biggest and only continent in Austrilasia. It's a western culture, primarly derived from Britain. The accent that it's most popular for is also from Britain. Austrilasia's religious beliefs are usually that of Christianity and Catholicism.

Music:

Australasia's music scene is usually famous for it's aboriginal influence and style. The didgeridoo is one popular Austrilasia instrument, as it helped create Indigenous music, an entirely new genre only native to Australia. Indigenous music is believed to have been around for 40,000 to 60,000 years, and is still extremely popular today.

Food:

Before Australia was offically colonized, the hunting of Emu, Kangaroos, and Wallabies was one of the most common ways of getting food. Austrilasia is also known for it's alcohol, mainly rum. In one Australian colony, Rum was used as a currency to exchange goods and services. Because of the geography and proximity of the sea, Australia has some of the most top rated sea food in the world.
