Micronesia

Culture

The culture of Micronesia mostly revolves around the way they live in the tropics, as well as their religious beliefs.

People and Languages

Micronesia has an estimated population of around 100,000 people. There are two major ethnic groups with almost 50% of the population being Chuukese, with one quarter being Pohnpeian.

The official language that is spoken in Micronesia is English, with eight different languages spoken in different parts of the islands. Those eight languages are Yapese, Ulithian, Woleaian, Chuukese, Pohnpeian, Kosraean, Nukuoro and Kapingamarangi.

Music

The main style of music in micronesia that people enjoy is contemporary/traditional. Contemporary music is the music that is very mainstream and that everyone listens to, while traditional music is the music that's performed as a custom, which are mainly folk songs. Along with folk songs, Micronesia has it's own set of folk dances.
